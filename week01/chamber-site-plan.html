Overview

Purpose

The Barinas Chamber of Commerce will be a tool used for the city to unite all the commerce in a single place given easier access to users for what they are looking for. Likewise we will give our members the opportunity to grow quicker by giving them different benefits for being part of our selected community.

Audience

Small/Medium/Big companies who want to develop/share/receive for the community

Scenarios

How did members part of the organization grow in the past month?

  • Automating processes of repetitive tasks.
  • Order brought to the companies allows sellers to pass more time selling, increasing their incomes by 7%.
  • Productive increased by more than 10% by giving well treatments and salaries

What are some of the benefits that the organization offers to members?

  • The most important benefit for members is the established networking, they'll be able to be part of meetings and events where they'll meet other managers and experts from different areas.
  • Our members will be in a constant learning process becoming part of enriching talks with other members, staff, or experts who have been already in the situations they are going through.
  • exclusive information sharing statistics, surveys, and studies made to the current market to understand the trend
